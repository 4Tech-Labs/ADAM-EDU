## Violation Contract

| Prefix | Meaning | Examples detected |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `CITA:` | External citation or academic/industry-source pattern in generated prose | `según el estudio`, `paper`, `et al.`, `(2023)` |
| `UNANCHORED:` | Model-metric numeric value in generated prose not present in the metrics block within tolerance | `87% de AUC` when only `auc_lr: 0.7100` is anchored |

Numeric anchoring accepts +/-2 percentage points for percentage-like values and +/-2% relative tolerance for scalar values. Business figures from M2, Exhibits, or M4 remain valid context and are not blocked by the model-metric validator. Structural references such as M1-M6 labels, section numbers, parenthetical years, non-metric date ranges, and fixed writing-rule phrases are stripped only for numeric anchoring; citation detection still runs on the raw prose first.
